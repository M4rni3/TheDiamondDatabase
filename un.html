The Mr. and Ms. UN Saga

What began as teachers trying to convince Jon and Rein to join the pageant (which they did succeed in) evolved into the chaos of trying to create their costumes to represent the country of Colombia.

With multiple "workshop" sessions in and out of the school to create the gown for Rein and the embellishments for Jon's clothes, we were able to successfully make what would eventually win "Best in National Costume".

Noteable happenings included Reynaldo posing as a "mannequin" for Rein's gown while the other "mannequins" were resting and just loads of time and gossip sunk into this but all worth the effort as our classmate eventually won EVERYTHING!
